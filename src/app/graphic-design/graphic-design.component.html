Cristiano Ronaldo (Juventus): Ronaldo had either scored or assisted in every one of Juventus' Serie A matches in 2020 as they march almost unopposed towards another Scudetto. He currently has five UEFA Champions Leagues to his name, Europe's toughest competition to win.

Robert Lewandowski (Bayern Munich): In 2020: 21 goals, four assists. Won Bundesliga & DFB Pokal. The Poland international has been in blistering form in 2020, having fired Bayern to a domestic double while helping make them the favourites to win the Champions League.

Neymar (Paris Saint-Germain): In 2020: Won the French Ligue 1 and led PSG to the Champions League final. Has 1 UEFA Champions League title to his name and 3 Ligue 1 titles.

Kylian Mbappe (Paris Saint-Germain): If you were to look at Kylian Mbappe's trophy cabinet, you wouldn't believe he is still only 21 years old. A World Cup, three Ligue 1 titles, and the Coupe de France are just a few of his collective honours. Individually, he's won the Golden Boy award, the Ligue 1 Player of the Season trophy, and Ligue 1's top scorer prize.
